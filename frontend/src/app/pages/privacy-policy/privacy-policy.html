Privacy Policy

At TransportHub, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our platform:

  • Personal Information: When you sign up or book a ticket, we collect personal details such as your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Usage Data: We may collect data on how you interact with the platform, such as pages visited, search queries, and booking history.
  • Payment Information: For payment processing, we collect billing details, including payment method information, but we do not store sensitive payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide, personalize, and improve our services, such as ticket booking, route recommendations, and customer support.
  • Process payments and manage transactions related to your bookings.
  • Send you important updates about your bookings or changes in services.
  • Enhance security and prevent fraudulent activities on our platform.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: We may share your data with trusted partners who help us operate the platform, such as payment processors and customer service teams.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, we may share information to comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

4. Security of Your Information

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no data transmission method over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information through your account settings.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails.
  • Request a copy of your data for review and correction.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and other tracking technologies to improve your experience and analyze usage patterns. Cookies help us remember your preferences and provide personalized content. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will update the "last modified" date at the bottom. Please review this policy periodically for the latest information.
